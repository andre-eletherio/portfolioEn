WhatsApp Replica

WhatsApp Replica

The preferred messaging application for Brazilians is WhatsApp, present in 99.2% of national smartphones. In addition, around 88% of its users access it every day.

With that in mind, I decided to recreate it from scratch. I developed the entire interface and messaging system, without using any kind of ready-made library. All logic and implementation came out of many attempts and improvements.

In the end, an interactive and functional messaging application was ready!

Pizza Delivery

Pizza Delivery

We all have had not so good experiences with the service of a restaurant, thinking about it, I developed this application.

It works as an interactive menu, where the user can select what he wants to eat, and then the order is sent to the restaurant's kitchen.

At the end, the order note is displayed, with the information necessary for the customer to monitor their expenses.

Rappi Replica
